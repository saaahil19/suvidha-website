Welcome to Suvidha

Since its inception on September 8, 1995, Suvidha Mahila Mandal, now known as Suvidha Foundation, has been a beacon of hope, dedicated to illuminating the lives of the underprivileged through education. Our mission is to ensure parity in education, fostering the strength of young minds to build a promising future.

Mission

  • Imparting Education: Bridging the educational divide by offering quality education to financially challenged children
  • Empowering Communities: Enhancing lives through social awareness, healthcare, and vocational training
  • Building a Sustainable Future: Promoting environmental consciousness with initiatives like tree plantation drives

Vision

We envision a world where everyone has access to equal opportunities, quality education, and a sustainable, healthy environment.

What We Do.

Suvidha provides assistance to people and communities in need and empowers them to become sustainable, independent and proud. How and where we contribute is based on the cultural challenges and strengths of a community as defined by its most precious resource-the people. We use your support to build schools, provide educational materials, create sustainable food programs and provide hope.
