About Us

Suvidha Mahila Mandal, established on September 8, 1995, is a non-profit organization working to impart education among the financially challenged sections to help them realize parity in education and the strength of little minds in building a promising future.

The organization has provisions for student internships, mentorship programs, and volunteering opportunities. Through these initiatives, Suvidha Foundation aims to achieve the vision of imparting innovative education that stays with students forever and equips them for the unforeseen future.

Our Mission

To inspire students, help them innovate, and let them integrate to build the next generation humankind.

To run food donation and awareness campaigns in rural regions.

Our Vision

To build the next generation of entrepreneurs by providing them with skill-based education.

To provide internships, training, workshops, and quality education all over the world.
